\subsection{dcsl\-\_\-miabot\-\_\-state\-\_\-estimator}\label{index_dcsl_miabot_state_estimator}
\-This node estimates the states (poses) of the robots based on incoming measurements from the vision tracker and control inputs from the low level controller. \-States are propagated and estimates published at a rate of at least 25 \-Hz (with more depending on rate of measurements).\subsection{\-Usage}\label{index_usage}
\begin{DoxyVerb}
$ dcsl_miabot_state_estimator [standard ROS args]
\end{DoxyVerb}
